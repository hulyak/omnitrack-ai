# OmniTrack AI - Vision

## Mission

Transform supply chains from reactive crisis management into proactive, self-healing networks powered by autonomous AI agents.

---

- 69% of companies experienced disruptions last year
- Detection time: 3-7 days

- Manual processes can't scale

**Why Current Solutions Fail:**
- Legacy systems are reactive
- Single-agent AI can't collaborate
- No real-time learning
- Limited explainability

---

## Our Solution

### Multi-Agent Architecture

Four specialized AI agents work together autonomously:

**1. Info Agent**
- Monitors IoT sensors and market signals
- Detects anomalies in real-time
- Predicts disruptions 7-14 days ahead

**2. Scenario Agent**
- Simulates thousands of outcomes
- Models "what-if" scenarios
- Evaluates cascading effects

**3. Strategy Agent**
- Recommends mitigation options
- Optimizes for cost, time, and risk
- Balances competing objectives

**4. Impact Agent**
- Assesses business consequences
- Quantifies trade-offs
- Tracks sustainability metrics

### Key Capabilities

- **Autonomous Operation**: 24/7 monitoring and response
- **Proactive Intelligence**: Predict and prevent, not react
- **Explainable AI**: Natural language explanations for every decision
- **Continuous Learning**: Improves from every outcome
- **Enterprise-Grade**: Built on AWS with security by design
